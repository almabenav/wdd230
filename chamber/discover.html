Medellin

Medellín is the second most important city in Colombia, and capital of the department of Antioquia; its average temperature is 24º and it is located 1,479 meters above sea level; it has an area of 105 square kilometers of urban land, 270 of rural land and 5.2 of land for expansion.

demography of medellin

According to 2018 census data from the National Administrative Department of Statistics -DANE-, as of that year Medellín had a population of 2,427,129 inhabitants, 47% men and 53% women, making it the second most populous city in Colombia.
