About us

Bountiful Food es una empresa que se especializa en alimentos orgánicos cultivados localmente, prinvipalmente en Carlsbad, California, sin embargo, cubrimos la zona costera desde Los Ángeles hasta San Diego!

Traemos productos organicos a tu mesa desde 2010, primero unicamente con una pequena tienda en Carlsbad, con el pasar de los anos hemos podido llegar a mas hogares y vidas

Purpose

Among our main objectives at Bountiful food are:

  • Provide people living in the city with organic food of the highest quality.
  • Offer a portfolio of products that contribute to improving the health of citizens of all ages.
  • Educate people of all ages and provide them with tools to make better consumer choices that positively impact their health and the health of those around them.
  • Promote organic production, respecting nature and the environment.
