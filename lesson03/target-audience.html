Target Audience

Natural persons, commercial and civil societies, companies, commercial establishments and branches that exercise commercial activities, from the age of 18 years and older, that are generating income or want to start generating it. It is expected to make a responsive page for users to access from any platform.

Personas

First persona

Fictional name: Jorge
Job title/major responsibilities: Entrepreneur
Demographics: 25 years, Single, with a Bachelor's degree
Goals and task: start and develop a new business, seeks to make contacts, advice and financing.

Second persona

Fictional name: Martha
Job title/major responsibilities: Bussiness Bachelor
Demographics: 35 years, Married, mother of two children
Goals and task: seeks to grow its business, seeks to increase its business network and sustainability.

Scenarios

  • Can I make virtual transactions?
  • Can I find here programs for the development of my business?
  • How can I find tools to grow my business in a simple way?
  • Can I make contacts and learn more about the community?
  • Can I find here the orgazinations in my city or sector?
